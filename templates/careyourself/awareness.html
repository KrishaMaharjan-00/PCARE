Awareness

Awareness

You must avoid the followings during your pregnancy. Intake or use of them can lead to different problems like miscarriage, under development of fetus, high risks for both mother and baby.

Awareness and Warnings

  1. Don’t use anything labeled “toxic”.
  2. Try natural cleaning products. They can be safer.
  3. Wear thick rubber gloves and open the windows and doors to get rid of the fumes.
  4. Stay away from nail salons when you’re pregnant. Chemicals in nail salons can let off dangerous gases.
  5. If you can’t get away, stay near an open window or door for fresh air.
  6. Dyes, permanents, and straighteners are generally safe. A very small amount of the chemical can go from your head into your body, but there are no reports that this is harmful to you or your baby.
  7. It’s best to avoid these products during the first trimester (weeks 1-12).
  8. Do not go in a hot tub that is hotter than 101-102 degrees.
  9. Do not stay in the hot tub for more than 10 minutes.
    10. Drink lots of water.
  10. If you feel hot, you probably need to get out!
